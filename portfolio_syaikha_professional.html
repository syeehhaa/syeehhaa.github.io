Advancing Hate Speech Detection in Indonesian Language Using Graph Neural Networks and TF-IDF
Syaikha Amirah Zikrina, Fitriyani · Telkom University · Published Feb 2025

Proposed a Graph Neural Network (GCN) approach with TF-IDF feature weighting to detect hate speech and abusive language in 13,169 manually labeled Indonesian tweets, building a similarity graph from cosine distances between TF-IDF vectors. The GNN model reached 92.90% accuracy for Abusive and 89.78% for Hate Speech, clearly ahead of an RNN baseline at 86.09% / 86.15%, showing the value of relational, graph-based representations for short, informal text.

Graph Neural Network TF-IDF RNN Indonesian NLP
IEEE ICADEIS 2025 doi.org/10.1109/ICADEIS65852.2025.10933470
Improving Cyberbullying Detection with Graph Neural Network Approaches
Syaikha Amirah Zikrina, Fitriyani · Telkom University · 2025

Compared Graph Convolutional Networks (GCN) and Graph Attention Networks (GAT) for cyberbullying detection on social media text, after text normalization and TF-IDF feature extraction, across training runs from 20 to 200 epochs. GCN consistently outperformed GAT, peaking at 94.87% accuracy and 91.39% F1-score versus GAT's 87.79% accuracy and 79.27% F1-score, with recall identified as GCN's strongest margin of advantage.
